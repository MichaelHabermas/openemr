## LDL 130 Follow-Up

LDL cholesterol greater than or equal to 130 mg/dL is a primary-care follow-up signal that should be reviewed with cardiovascular risk factors and treatment history.

## Statin Risk Discussion

Cholesterol follow-up commonly includes review of age, diabetes status, ASCVD history, smoking, blood pressure, and current lipid-lowering therapy.

## Lipid Repeat Testing

After lifestyle or medication changes for cholesterol management, repeat lipid measurement is commonly used to assess adherence and response.

## LDL Evidence Boundary

This excerpt supports retrieval of guideline context for LDL follow-up. It does not by itself recommend a medication change for an individual patient.
