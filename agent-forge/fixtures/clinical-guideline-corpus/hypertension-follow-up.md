## Elevated Blood Pressure Confirmation

Elevated office blood pressure should be confirmed with repeat measurement and clinical context before treatment decisions are made.

## Hypertension Follow-Up

Primary care blood pressure follow-up commonly considers recent readings, cardiovascular risk, kidney disease, diabetes, medication adherence, and home measurements.

## Severe Blood Pressure Boundary

Very high blood pressure with concerning symptoms requires urgent clinical assessment rather than routine guideline-only follow-up.

## Blood Pressure Evidence Boundary

This excerpt supports context retrieval for blood pressure follow-up and does not replace clinician diagnosis or emergency assessment.
