## Colorectal Screening

Adults in the usual-risk screening age range should have colorectal cancer screening status reviewed during primary care follow-up.

## Depression Screening

Primary care follow-up commonly includes screening for depression when systems are in place for diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up.

## Tobacco Use Screening

Primary care visits should identify tobacco use and connect patients who use tobacco with evidence-based cessation support.

## Preventive Screening Evidence Boundary

Preventive screening guidance must be applied with patient age, history, prior results, and clinician judgment.
